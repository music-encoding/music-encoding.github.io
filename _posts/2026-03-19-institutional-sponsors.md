### Institutional Membership Sponsor Levels 
The [MEI By-Laws](https://music-encoding.org/community/mei-by-laws.html) have been updated to reflect the revised language about Institutional Memberships in section 3.1.2 that was voted on by the membership. Learn more about the three levels of institutional membership: [Regular, Sponsor, and Patron members](https://music-encoding.org/community/institutional-sponsorship.html).

If you are part of an organization or project that may be interested in becoming an institutional sponsor of MEI, please reach out to the MEI Board (mei-board@lists.uni-paderborn.de).

### Community Forum
The slides from the March 10th [MEI Community Forum](https://music-encoding.org/community/community-forums.html) are now available on the Community Forum page. You will also find slides from past forums on this page. If you have questions or feedback for anyone who provided updates at the forum, please reach out to them directly.
